Who

A Reader
of Symbols

Tegan found her first deck years ago and saw her own story in the cards. Since then she has dedicated herself to learning the language of tarot — not as prophecy, but as a mirror for the unconscious.

For her, every reading is a conversation. The cards surface what is already known inside; she simply holds the light so you can see clearly.

She works with Rider-Waite-Smith, Thoth, and carefully chosen independent decks. Every session is unique — she never reads the same way twice.
